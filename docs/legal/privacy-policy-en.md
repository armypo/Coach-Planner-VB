## 3. Information We Collect

Playco collects only the information strictly necessary for the app to function:

| Category | Data | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Identification | Username (firstname.lastname), hashed password (SHA‑256 with salt), role (coach / athlete / admin) | Account creation |
| Profile | First name, last name, profile photo, jersey number, position, physical data (height, weight, reach, vertical) | User input |
| Sports data | Match statistics (kills, aces, blocks, digs, sets, receives), physical tests, training sessions | Input by coach or athlete |
| Educational content | Drills, strategies, coach notes, court diagrams | User input |
| Communication | Team messages, private conversations | User input |
| Team affiliation | Team code, institution name | Team creation or join |

**Playco does not collect:**
- Your personal email (optional for coach profile only)
- Payment information (the app is free)
- GPS location
- Your contacts
- Browsing history
- Advertising or tracking data

---

## 4. Purposes of Collection

We use your information solely to:

1. **Operate the application**: authentication, data display, team management
2. **Synchronize your data across devices** via iCloud (Apple CloudKit)
3. **Enable team communication**
4. **Archive your sports history** during your use of the app

We **never** use your data for advertising, profiling, or resale.

---

## 5. Hosting and Storage

Playco uses **Apple iCloud (CloudKit)** infrastructure:

- Your data is stored locally on your device (SwiftData)
- It is synchronized with your personal iCloud account
- Storage and encryption are managed by **Apple Inc.** under the [iCloud terms](https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/icloud/)
- No data is sent to servers operated by Origo Technologies

We do not transfer your data outside Canada or the United States (depending on your Apple ID region). Apple manages this infrastructure according to its own privacy commitments.

---

## 6. Retention Period

- **Throughout your use** of Playco
- **Up to 30 days** after account deletion to prevent accidental recovery
- Archived data (past matches, completed seasons) remains available as long as your team exists

## 9. Security

We take the following measures to protect your information:

- SHA-256 with salt hashing for passwords (never stored in plain text)
- Encryption in transit and at rest via Apple iCloud
- Automatic lockout after 5 failed login attempts
- No proprietary Origo servers: no external attack vector

In case of a security incident affecting your data, we will notify you in accordance with Section 3.5 of Law 25.

## 10. Minors Under 14

Playco is designed for use in a school or college sports context. For users **under 14 years old**, parental, legal guardian, or school representative consent (e.g., RSEQ, school coach) is required.

If you are a parent or guardian and discover that a child under 14 has created an account without your authorization, contact us for immediate deletion.
